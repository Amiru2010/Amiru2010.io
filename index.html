Ananda College

Incepted as a result of the Sri Lankan Buddhist Renaissance which took place in the late 19th century, Ananda College continues to be a premier boys’ school in the country, producing countless Sri Lankan erudite scholars, professionals, military servicemen and all around well-disciplined citizens.

Founder

Colonel Henry Steel Olcott.

Colonel Henry Steel Olcott was an American military officer, journalist, lawyer, Freemason and the co-founder and first president of the Theosophical Society. Olcott was the first well-known American of European ancestry to make a formal conversion to Buddhism
